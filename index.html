About Our Masjid

Established in 1968, Town Jama Masjid has been the spiritual heart of Rahiman Nagar and Jaigaon for over five decades.

Our mission is to serve the Muslim community through religious guidance, educational programs, and social services.

We welcome everyone to join us in prayers, learning, and community activities.
