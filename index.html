About Our Masjid

Established in 1968, Town Jama Masjid has been the spiritual heart of Rahiman Nagar and Jaigaon for over five decades. We are more than just a place of worship - we are a vibrant center for community development, Islamic education, and social upliftment.

Our mission is to serve the Muslim community through religious guidance, educational programs, and social services, fostering unity and spiritual growth while preserving our Islamic heritage.

Join us in building a stronger, more connected Muslim society rooted in faith and service.
